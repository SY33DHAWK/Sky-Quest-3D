A high-quality arcade flight game with modest graphics and atmospheric effects.

Stunning Visuals:

> Realistic procedural sky with atmospheric gradient
> Volumetric clouds with depth
> Dynamic lighting with sun and ambient effects
> Smooth antialiasing and fog effects
> Particle systems for engine trails and effects

Exciting Gameplay:

> Fly a stylized 3D airplane through endless skies
> Navigate through golden rings to score points (100 points each)
> Avoid mountain peaks and cloud barriers
> Speed increases progressively for growing challenge
> Smooth arcade-style flight physics

Responsive Controls:

> Arrow Keys or WASD: Control airplane direction
> Spacebar: Boost for extra speed
> P or Escape: Pause game
> R: Restart after game over

Polish & Details:

> Animated propeller rotation
> Banking/tilting aircraft when turning
> Score tracking with high score memory
> Real-time HUD showing speed, altitude, and distance
> Explosion effects on collision
> Clean UI with start and game over screens
